RPi5 + Pixhawk 6C 實機通訊整合

工程級 Offboard 模式配置與 Mediapipe 手勢控制實作

1

硬體層級連線 (Physical Link)

RPi 5 GPIO

  • Pin 8 (TXD0) → Pixhawk RX
  • Pin 10 (RXD0) → Pixhawk TX
  • Pin 6 (GND) → Common Ground

Pixhawk 6C (TELEM2)

  • Baudrate: 921600
  • Protocol: MAVLink 2.0
  • Logic Level: 3.3V TTL
2

Offboard 模式代碼實作

async def run_offboard():
    drone = System()
    # RPi5 串口路徑
    await drone.connect(system_address="serial:///dev/ttyAMA0:921600")
    
    # 必須先發送 Setpoint 指令才能切換模式
    await drone.offboard.set_velocity_body(VelocityBodyYawspeed(0,0,0,0))
    await drone.offboard.start()
